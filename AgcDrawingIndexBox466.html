This page provides links to Project Apollo electrical and mechanical drawings from one particular NARA SW aperture-card box.  Background information about the scanned drawings and the engineering-drawing indices are provided on the electro-mechanical page.  Note the following related resources as well:

  • Browse scans of engineering drawings from the "aperture card" boxes from NARA SW.
  • Miscellaneous engineering-drawing index page.  These are alternate scans of some of the drawings from the NARA aperture-card boxes mentioned above, plus a number of drawings (or revisions of drawings) missing from the aperture-card boxes.
  • You can alternatively search for drawing numbers or drawing titles.
  • Or you can browse the drawings on an AGC/DSKY assembly-by-assembly basis.
  • Many of the AGC/DSKY electrical schematics are available in the form of CAD files, accessible by drawing number/revision.  (Explanation.)
  • CAD files for electrical schematics have been rendered as image files, which are generally much easier and pleasanter to view or print than the scans of the original drawings.
