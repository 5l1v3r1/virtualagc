5.21 MLDD Press and release OP4, OP3, OP2, OP1, OA3, SIGN and 1 through 12 pushbutton/lamps.
These instructions are based on the notion that 2 indicator lamps like COMPUTER-OP4 and COMMAND-OP4 are combined on a single pushbutton labeled OP4. In the emulation, the “COMPUTER” ones are pure indicator lamps, while the “COMMAND” ones are in fact combined indicator+pushbutton. E.g., where the instructions say to press OP4, in the emulation this should be read as pressing COMMAND-OP4.
