This page provides links to miscellaneous Apollo Lunar Module electrical and mechanical drawings collected outside of the systematic effort to scan LEM aperture-card boxes from the U.S. National Archives.  The scans on this page include primarily alternate scans of drawings whose initial scans from the National Archives were illegible for one reason or another, along with some scans made available from sources other than the National archives.  Background information about the scanned drawings and the engineering-drawing indices are provided on the electro-mechanical page.
