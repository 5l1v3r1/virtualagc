This page is a portal that allows you to navigate through the engineering-drawing tree for whatever AGC or DSKY models you may choose ... to the extent that we actually have those drawings, of course!  The installation lists mentioned in the table include all of the guidance-system equipment for the configuration, such as the IMU and CDU, though to date we have concentrated on acquiring AGC and DSKY engineering drawings and hopefully will add others in the future.  Or you may be able to find some of the others by consulting our MIT/IL engineering-drawing index.   However, for the AGC or DSKY, simply click on the desired part-number link in the table below to see its drawing tree.
