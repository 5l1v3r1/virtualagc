Search Results

For the displayed results, note that the titles of the results follow the pattern

NNNNNNNR-T-S-F

  • NNNNNNN is the 7-digit drawing number.
  • R is the revision code:  -, A, B, ..., Z, AA, AB, ....
  • T is the document type:  1 for drawings or 2 for acceptance & test requirements documents.
  • S is the sheet number
  • F is the frame number, for sheets that were too big for a single microfilm slide.

The descriptive text in the search result is the actual title from the drawing's title block.
