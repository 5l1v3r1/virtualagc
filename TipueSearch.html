 Extra search tricks:
  • Use a dash before a word to exclude all results with that word in it.  Example: -DSKY
  • Use enclosing quotes (single or double) to search for an exact phrase.  Example:  "TIMER MODULE"
  • Use enclosing quotes to search for a specific drawing number.  Examples:
    • "2005003-" finds specific revision 2005003-
    • "2005003A" finds specific revision 2005003A
    • "2005003" finds all revisions, 2005003-, 2005003A, 2005003B, etc.
