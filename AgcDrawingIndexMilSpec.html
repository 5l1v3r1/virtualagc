Drawing Index

This section provides links to mil-spec parts referenced in Apollo G&N engineering drawings that I've downloaded from public sources, particularly the U.S. government ASSIST site.

Only drawings free of copyright restrictions are provided here at Virtual AGC.  The most prominent examples of copyright-restricted drawings needed for the Apollo G&N systems, but which I've had to exclude, are the NASxxxx specs from the Aerospace Industries Association.  In some cases NASxxxx specs supercede or are superceded by MSxxxx mil specs.  In those few cases I have added the parts to the table below, but the hyperlinks are to the associated MSxxxx spec rather than to the NASxxxx spec itself ... a procedure which I admit is questionable.  Here is a list of some NASxxxx specs for which I've not been able to identify associated MSxxxx specs at all:  NAS145, NAS620, NAS662, NAS671, NAS674, NAS1219, NAS1394, NAS1402, NAS1515, NAS1598.  You can probably probably read all of the NASxxxx specs online by googling for them, even if I cannot legally post them here.
